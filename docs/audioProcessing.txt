Audio Context:
Represents an Audio Processing Graph (graph DS) which consists of connected AudioNode(s).
It controls both creation of the nodes and execution of audio processing.              

More: https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/AudioContext


Audio Node: Interface for representing audio processing module. It could represent:
- Audio Source (HTML audio, video , OscillatorNode etc)
- Audio Destination
- Intermediate Processing module (like an audio filter)
- Volume control: like GainNode

More: https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/AudioNode
